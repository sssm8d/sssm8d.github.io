Solid State Final Paper Visualizations

See cool visualizations of the ferroelectric domain walls.

71 deg domain

Visualization of 71 deg polarization shift between domains. The domain wall is oriented along the (011) plane. Polarization one one side of domain wall has orientation of [111]. Polarization on other side of domain wall has orientation of [{-1}11].

109 deg domain

Visualization of 109 deg polarization shift between domains. The domain wall is oriented along the (001) plane. Polarization one side of domain wall has orientation of [111]. Polarization other side of domain wall has orientation of [{-1}{-1}1].

180 deg domain

Visualization of 180 deg polarization shift between domains. The domain wall is oriented along the (0{-1}1) plane. Polarization one side of domain wall has orientation of [111]. Polarization other side of domain wall has orientation of [{-1}{-1}{-1}].
